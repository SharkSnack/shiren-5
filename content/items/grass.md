# Details

#### How Grasses Work

- Use a grass or seed item to apply the item's effect to Shiren.
    - In addition, all grass and seed items replenish 5 fullness.
- Throw a grass or seed item at a creature to apply the item's effect to that creature.

#### Locations Key

|Letter|Meaning|
|-|-|
|F|Floor, Monster drop (day)|
|S|Store, Peddler, Shiny Object (yellow), Monster drop (night), Maneater drop|
|P|Presto Pot|
|Z|Zalokleft drop|
|M|Mealy drop|
|E|Elite store, Pick-A-Choice, Shiny Object (yellow, blue)|

### Weeds

No effect when eaten, other than restoring 5 fullness like all other grass items.<br/>
Increases action speed of Field Knave family monsters by 1 stage if thrown at them.

Obtained from Field Knaves (special attack or drop), used Revival Grass or Undo Grass,<br/>
allies getting hit by Swordsman special attacks, or from a Secret Pot after 8 days pass.

Synthesize with a weapon to create the Anti-Plant rune.

### Herb

Restores 25 HP, or increases max HP by 1 if already at max.<br/>
It's best to wait until your HP is full to increase max HP early ~ mid game,<br/>
but it's fine to use it right away late game, since you don't have time to step in place.

An Herb is placed where a New Item would've been generated when<br/>
you don't have any New Items registered to your encyclopedia.

Synthesize with a weapon or shield to create the Lively rune.

Increases level by 3. (6 if blessed)<br/>
Useful for leveling up ally characters.<br/>
It's best to duplicate them using Fever Pots, and then create New Items out of them.<br/>
You could also synthesize it to increase your weapon or shield's base stat by 8.<br/>
Madam Ateska can force it to appear on the ground in select dungeons.

Synthesize with a weapon or shield to create the Augmenting rune.

### SuperUnlucky Seed

Reduces level and current HP to 1, and sets fullness to 0.<br/>
However, eating grass replenishes 5 fullness, so you won't collapse right away.<br/>
Be mindful that items dropped by night monsters are selected from the shop table.<br/>
It's a disasterous item, but the effect can be nullified by a Staunch Bracelet.

Synthesize with a weapon or shield to create the Perilous rune.
