Details

How Scrolls Work

  • Read a scroll to cause an effect to occur.
  • Some scrolls like Sanctuary Scroll should be placed on the ground instead.
  • Extinction Scroll should be thrown at a monster instead.

Oil Scroll

Place it on the ground, and the enemy who steps on it will be inflicted with Tottering status.
If the enemy is a Floating type monster, it will deal 50 damage instead. (100 if blessed)
Tottering status doubles damage received and lasts 6 turns. (12 turns if blessed)

Basically a trap that can be used without a Trapper Bracelet, but placing it ends your turn.
Use a Swap Staff while standing on it to inflict Tottering status on the same turn.
Effective against bosses, but Flamebirds will be healed by the flame.

Synthesize this + Upgrade Seed + Explosion Bracelet with a sword for the Blue Flame rune.

Blessing Scroll

Blesses a selected item. (cursed or sealed items will change to blessed)
This and Exorcism Scroll are the only items able to remove seals from pots.
Revival Grass and Undo Grass are popular targets to gain extra lives,

Keep in mind that projectiles can't be blessed.

If you obtain 2 Blessing Scrolls, bless one of them to save inventory space.
If one of them is sealed, bless the sealed scroll to get 2 uses out of it.
Famous for being the "cheat item" of Shiren 5.
