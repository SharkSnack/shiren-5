Knockback Staff

Deals 5 damage and knocks a target away.
Monsters will be sent 7 tiles, items and traps 10 tiles, statues 3 tiles, and stairs until it hits a wall.
Monsters that land on traps will trigger them, provided Shiren has the Trapper status.
Note that pots will shatter when they're sent flying.
Found with 4~7 uses.

Possession Staff

Lets you take control of a monster, allowing you to use their normal and special attacks.
Shiren's body will follow behind the monster, so you'll want to protect it as you move around.
You can return to your body mostly anytime, and you'll gain exp for enemies you defeated when you do so.
Can only take control of monsters that can be registered in Monster Kingdom.
Found with 4~6 uses.

Swap places with a target. Doesn't work on statues.
Traps will be triggered if the destination tile has a trap on the ground.
This is also the case for monsters, provided Shiren has Trapper status.

Balance Staff

Protects the holder from tripping, but it doesn't work if it's inside of a pot.
Throw or swing it at a monster to make them trip and drop an item.
Plays well with a Pierce Bracelet when used in this way.
Monsters won't trip if a No-Magic statue is present, or if they're asleep from a Slumber Scroll.
Found with 0~1 uses.

Monarch Tuskers carry this staff and have a 3% chance of dropping one when defeated.

Seal Staff

Prevents a target from dividing or using special abilities.
Also resets action speed if the target is not Shiren.
Some monster abilities cannot be sealed, such as floating or fire-immunity.
If Shiren is the target, he won't be able to eat, drink, or read for 35 turns.
King Tuskers carry this staff and have a 3% chance of dropping one when defeated.
Found with 3~6 uses.

Miss Staff

Lowers a target's direct attack accuracy to 0% for 21~31 turns.
Doesn't affect accuracy of special attacks or projectiles.
Attacks will always connect regardless of this staff's effect if a target is sleeping, paralyzed,
or caught in a bear trap, so use a different item against Monarch Tuskers and Stun Scorpions.

Fire Staff

Creates a line of fire 5 tiles forward that deals 20 damage per turn.
Any items or statues along the path will burn up.
Non-piercing projectiles will also burn up if they fly into the flames.
Plays well with Knockback Staff, and can be used to get rid of Sanctaruy Scrolls on the ground.
Found with 4~7 uses.
