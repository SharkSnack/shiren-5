# Item

#### Wet Scroll + Fever Pot

Wet Scrolls can be duplicated by Fever Pots, even if the scrolls were different before becoming wet.<br/>The resulting Wet Scrolls will match the first Wet Scroll you insert into the pot.

Example:

1. Insert a Wet Scroll (Blank Scroll)
2. Insert a Wet Scroll (Exorcism Scroll)
3. Insert a Wet Scroll (Vacuum Slash Scrl)
4. Fever Pot breaks, obtain 6 Wet Scrolls (Blank Scroll)

How to turn scrolls wet:

- Throw a scroll at a Mixer.
- Splash water on a scroll using a Water Pot.
- Equip a Waterwalk Bracelet and walk on water tiles.
- Tsunami dungeon event (DS version only)

#### 2 Item Fever Pot

The minimum capacity where a Fever Pot duplicates items is actually 2 instead of 3.<br/>This is useful when you want to duplicate very rare items like True Knife or Nirvana Board.

How to obtain a Fever Pot[2]:

1. Have Gyandora or Gyandoron peck a Fever Pot, reducing the pot's capacity to [0].
2. Read 2 Pot God Scrolls on the Fever Pot to increase its capacity to [2].

The quickest way to find a Gyandora is to level up a Gyadon in Tower of Fortune (2-3F).<br/>You'll either need a shield without the <span class="greenText">Anti-Peck</span> rune, or a Klein Pot and a strong weapon.

#### Dirt in Pots

Have Scoopie fill pots like Ordinary Pot, Sale Pot, or Sticky Pot with dirt to obtain grass items.<br/>Black Hole Pot (dirt vanishes), Fever Pot (explodes), and Hide Pot (can't be filled) won't work.

#### Presto / Zalokleft Pot in Villages

Presto Pot and Zalokleft Pot have limited item tables in village warehouses.<br/>This makes it easy to stock up on Escape Scrolls.

- Nekomaneki Village - Onigiri, Escape Scroll
- Hermit's Hermitage - Torch, Fine Torch, Escape Scroll
- Sparrow's Inn - Fine Torch, Escape Scroll

#### No Damage Itemphobic

Step on an item and insert it into a pot instead of picking it up normally to avoid Itemphobic damage.<br/>If you insert it into a Preservation Pot, you can then take the item out and use it normally.

#### Selling Sale Pot in Villages

When selling Sale Pots in villages, the Sale Pot's value includes items inside the pot.<br/>This also applies to any Gitan bags that are inside the pot.

#### Max Number of Floor Items

The max number of items that can be on the ground on a floor is 128 items.<br/>If you throw or try to place an item when the limit has been reached, the item will vanish.

#### Quick Hitting Rune

The Quick Hitting rune only has a chance to activate when your attack would hit another character.<br/>It's not limited to adjacent direct attacks - It can activate with <span class="greenText">Tri-direction</span> and <span class="greenText">Flame Shot</span>.

#### Reusable Torches

Equipped torches can be used again if you throw them at Mixer type monsters.

#### Fragrance Pot Colors

- Dodger - <span class="highlightGreen">Green</span>
- Reflection - <span class="highlightPurple">Purple</span>
- Zen - <span class="highlightBlue">Blue</span>
- Perceptive - <span class="highlightYellow">Yellow</span>

#### Skill Point Boost

If you defeat the same monster consecutively, a multiplier gets added to the skill points you earn.<br/>Details are unknown, but it's particularly noticeable when hunting Maneater type monsters.

# Dungeon

#### Map Patterns

Dungeon maps change each time you go on an adventure, but some maps use fixed templates.<br/>Example: CHUNSOFT maps, where the generated map is shaped as one of the letters in "CHUNSOFT".<br/>See [Map Patterns](/system/map-patterns) for more information.

#### Night in Destiny Trail

Time of day doesn't naturally cycle in Destiny Trail or Tower of the Past/Present/Future.<br/>However, you can read a Night-Day Scroll to change time of day to night, which lasts for 5 turns.<br/>Time of day returns to daytime when you advance floors.

#### Lava + Monster House

As long as 1 monster is still inside the Monster House, lava won't erupt inside the room.<br/>If you want to take your time collecting items, consider paralyzing a monster.

#### No Ground Tiles Left

If you bury all ground tiles using a Fort. Staff and then unequip a Waterwalk or Floating bracelet<br/>while standing on a water tile, you won't warp anywhere, and you won't be able to move.
