# Details

#### How Scrolls Work

- Read a scroll to cause an effect to occur.<br/>
- Some scrolls like Sanctuary Scroll should be placed on the ground instead.<br/>
- Extinction Scroll should be thrown at a monster instead.

#### Locations Key

|Letter|Meaning|
|-|-|
|F|Floor, Monster drop (day)|
|S|Store, Peddler, Shiny Object (yellow), Monster drop (night), Maneater drop|
|P|Presto Pot|
|Z|Zalokleft drop|
|M|Mealy drop|
|E|Elite store, Pick-A-Choice, Shiny Object (yellow, blue)|

### Wet Scroll

No effect when read.<br/>
Chance to dry off and return to normal when you change floors. (must not be in a pot)<br/>
Created by walking on water tiles with a Waterwalk Bracelet, using a Water Pot,<br/>
Mixer swallowing a thrown scroll, or getting caught in a Tsunami (DS version).<br/>
Can be used with a Fever Pot to duplicate a single scroll into 6 scrolls.

### Muzzled Scroll

Inflicts Shiren with Muzzled status for the duration of the current floor.<br/>You can't use scrolls, grass, food, peaches, or abilities, and you can't talk to NPCs.<br/>Don't read unidentified 800G scrolls in shops, since you can't pay if it's this scroll.<br/>Mutaikon's grass throw won't replenish fullness, but Grass Kid's throw will do so.

Can be blocked by Immune status, or cured by the Chakra Cleanse ability at night.<br/>
Otherwise, it's best to hurry to the next floor if you accidentally read it.

You won't gain any experience points from reading this scroll.<br/>
Items that Mixers swallowed will vanish with the monster.

Obtained by giving a Blank Scroll to the Calligrapher after reading all other scrolls.

### Blank Scroll

Write the name of a scroll you've read before, and use this as that scroll.<br/>
No effect when read before writing the name of a scroll, and can't go in Secret Pots.<br/>
Unidentified scrolls of the same name will be automatically identified after writing.<br/>
You can't write on Blank Scrolls that have price tags, and you also can't write on<br/>
sealed Blank Scrolls - but attempting to do so reveals disguised N'dubbas.<br/>
Writing on the scroll doesn't end your turn.

Friendship Licenses can change into Blank Scrolls when you change floors.<br/>
Found with Angel Seeds when you receive a Super Good Madam Ateska fortune.<br/>
Can be given to the Calligrapher to obtain a scroll you haven't read before,<br/>
or an Expulsion Scroll if you've read every scroll in the game.<br/>
Available in Nekomaneki Village's Point Shop for 200 points.

### Extinction Scroll

Throw at a monster to make that monster family vanish and stop spawning.<br/>
If a second scroll is thrown, the first monster family will begin to appear again.<br/>
The effect lasts for the duration of the current adventure.<br/>
Shiren will collapse if he gets hit by the scroll.

Maneaters and Shopkeepers will vanish from the floor, but won't stop spawning.<br/>
Unlike Shiren 4, the scroll doesn't have piercing properties when thrown.<br/>
No effect when read, but it's highly recommended to read the first one<br/>
you obtain so that you can write it on a Blank Scroll.
