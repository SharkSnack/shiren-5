Gorger's Manor

Edit on GitHub

Dungeon where you can find lots of Special Onigiri on the ground.
That sounds lackluster, but it's actually an excellent dungeon to visit when you need points.
Rush to 47F and read 4 x Trap Scrolls → Trap Deletion Scrl, then step on Point Switches and repeat.
However, you'll want to bring strong equipment and useful items to deal with tougher monsters.
Overall, aside from point collection, this dungeon is mostly forgettable.
