# Overview

The encyclopedia keeps a record of items you've obtained and monsters you've defeated.<br/>Each entry has its own visual preview and description, some of which can be pretty humorous.

There are a total of 900 items, 468 monsters, and 79 music tracks.<br/>As you can imagine, this is going to take serious effort to reach 100% completion.
