Details

How Pots Work

  • Pots apply an effect to inserted items.
    • There are also pots that apply an effect when opened.
  • The capacity is generally random when you find a pot.
    • Max capacity is 5.
  • Pots shatter when thrown at a character or wall, and contents fall to the ground.
    • If an item that was inside the pot lands on a trap, the trap will activate.

4-2-8 Pot

Causes a big explosion to occur when it shatters. (1 tile radius)
Sealing the pot will prevent it from exploding, allowing you to safely retrieve contents.
Shiren's current HP will be reduced to 1, and all enemies caught in the blast vanish.

One of the few items that can one-shot Gyazas.

The name is a reference to 428: Shibuya Scramble.

Fever Pot

Fill the pot with same name items to double the number of items. (The pot shatters)
Explodes if different items are inserted, so be wary of Bored Kappas and Scoopies.
Unlike 4-2-8 Pot, other Fever Pots won't be identified when one breaks or explodes.
Always has an initial capacity of 3, and mostly found in shops.

Commonly used to duplicate rare items like Wonder Pick and Angel Seed.
See Tips and Tricks to learn about duplication techniques.

It's important to learn the details about how it works to avoid item loss:

Same NameRunes, upgrade value, tag, blessing, curse, seal are ignored.
Same type but different name equipment will fail. (example: Ordinary Stick + Ogre's Club)
Post-DuplicationRunes, upgrade value, tag, blessing, curse, seal are reset.
Staves, talismans, arrows, and rocks will have a random use count or quantity
in the same initial ranges that naturally generated items on the ground have.
Pot CapacityItems won't duplicate with a capacity of 1, but will duplicate with a capacity of 2.
Duplicating with a capacity of 2 will result in 4 items.

Reflection Pot

Insert an item to create a purple fragrance that reflects magic.
The effect applies to every creature on the current floor, and things like staves,
DJ Mage magic, Gazer hypnosis, and Polygon Spinna dances will be reflected.
However, Fearabbit's hat trick isn't considered magic, so it won't be reflected.
The fragrance remains in effect even if you collapse.

The drawback of not being able to use staves regularly is significant, but it allows
for some techniques like warping Shiren to the stairs using a Transient Staff,
or granting Swift status to yourself using a Swift Staff, etc.

Zalokleft Pot

Releases a Zalokleft that fetches an item in front, excluding pots and Catstones.
Its range is 10 tiles in a straight line, and it can travel across water tiles
but stops if it hits a pot, wall, or air tile. Break the pot to retrieve fetched items.
If it shatters when capacity is above 0, Confused Zaloklefts equal to capacity appear.

Using an Extraction Scroll on a Zalokleft Pot full of items won't restore capacity.
All other Zalokleft Pots will be identified if you break an unidentified one.
The Zalokleft won't come out of the pot if it's opened inside a shop,
so you'll have to open it from a hallway to steal items.

Monster Pot

A number of hostile monsters equal to capacity jump out when the pot is opened.
The monsters won't act on the turn they jumped out unless they're naturally Swift.
All other Monster Pots will be identified if you break an unidentified one.
The pot vanishes when opened, so keep that in mind when shopping.
If thrown against a wall, the monsters will have Confused status.

Use it to generate Grass Boys to obtain some extra grass items,
increase your defeated enemy count to activate Super status / gain bonuses,
or activate the Cross formation in a hallway intersection to replenish abilities, etc.
If opened in storage buildings, Bashaggas, Doom Gyazas, and Despoilers appear.

Hilarious Pot

Inflicts Laughing status on a target in front of you for 6 turns.
The laughter is contagious and travels to other monsters in the room as turns pass.
Shutting down a room in exchange for 1 use is good, but it doesn't last long,
and every enemy in the room stops laughing when one of them stops.
Can be thrown for the same effect if the pot's capacity is above 0.

Useful for waking up Monster Houses before entering them in Onigiri Hollow.
This is the only "Open" type pot that can have an inital capacity of 2.
The pot can work through corners, so there's no need to throw it.
Swift monsters will only laugh for 3 turns, so defeat them first.
