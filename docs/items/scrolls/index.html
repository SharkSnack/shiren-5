  • If a scroll gets splashed by water, it will turn into a Wet Scroll.
    • They can't be read, and won't have an effect when placed on the ground.
    • They will dry off when you advance floors, as long as they're not inside a pot.
    • Wet Scrolls stored inside Mamo will dry off when you advance floors.
    • Sort your inventory to get an idea what the Wet Scroll is if you have multiple copies of that scroll.
  • Some scrolls prompt for a selection when read.
    • No effect on items inside a pot.
    • You can cancel the prompt without losing the scroll or ending the turn.
    • The prompt appears even if Shiren is currently sealed.
  • Some scrolls only have an effect when placed on the ground.
    • The scroll must be stuck to the ground for it to activate.
    • Stuck scrolls can't be read, picked up, moved by Knockback Staff, etc.
    • Water Striders and Bored Kappas can pick them up, but Traders can't.
    • It'll remain stuck even if it becomes wet and the effect deactivates. (Excluding Blank Scrolls)
    • If the scroll is dropped by Shiren being tripped, it'll stick to the ground.
    • If a defeated monster drops a scroll, it won't stick to the ground.
      This means you can recover stuck scrolls by having Water Striders pick them up.
    • Stuck scrolls will revert to their original form in Napus Village's storage when you re-enter.

the first monster will start appearing again.
