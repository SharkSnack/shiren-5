Details

How Projectiles Work

  • Projectiles are primarily used to attack enemies from a distance.
  • Arrows have long range and deal damage based on Shiren's attack power.
  • Rocks deal fixed damage and can hit targets inside walls.

High attack power, and hits harder than a direct attack with a Katana.

Poison Arrow

Inflicts Weakened status on a target up to 10 tiles away in a straight line.
One arrow reduces their attack by 50%, making it effective against power monsters.

Knockback Arrow

Deals damage up to 10 tiles away, and occasionally knocks the target back.
The knockback effect is extremely powerful, and lets you defeat enemies without risk.
It's best to collect Knockback Arrows from a Strong Cart using a Dodger Pot.
You can never have enough of these arrows, and a set of 99 Knockback Arrows
can be sold for a little over 5000G if you're low on Gitan.

Truestrike Arrow

Deals damage to a target up to 10 tiles away in a straight line, and never misses.
The arrow always hits its target, even if you have an Inacc. Bracelet equipped,
a Dodger Pot is in effect, or a Bored Kappa throws the arrow back at you.

Projectiles normally have 84% accuracy, so having a reliable option is nice.
But these arrows are rare, so it's usually better to use inventory space
on sets of wood, iron, poison, or knockback arrows.

Rightstone

Deals 12 damage to a target up to 3 tiles away to the right. (Hits targets inside walls)
Useful for defeating Cave Mamel / Gitan Mamel in one hit and preventing Explochins
from exploding, but tricky to use since it only hits targets to the right.
FO-Uβ and night monsters only take 1 damage.

The stone has piercing properties and vanishes even if it misses its target,
and the flying to the right rule also applies to Bored Kappas.
